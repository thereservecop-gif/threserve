The Reserve – Member Payment Consent

The Reserve is a structured food allocation system designed to help members secure their December food supply early before seasonal price increases.

This program allows members to gradually prepare their household food supply across the year so they do not have to buy everything in December when prices are at their highest.

Important Program Rules

  • The Reserve runs on a 10-month cycle (March – December).
  • This program is not a savings scheme, thrift (ajo), or investment platform.
  • Members contribute so food items can be secured early before price increases.
  • Allocation Lock Date: End of Q2.
  • Before the Lock Date: refunds allowed minus a 10% administrative and allocation reversal fee.
  • After the Lock Date: no cash refunds.
  • Members who stop contributing will receive proportional food allocation based on the total amount contributed.
  • Food distribution occurs during the December delivery window.
  • Completed members receive doorstep delivery within Lagos.
  • Equivalent product substitutions may occur if market supply conditions change.

Don’t buy December food at December prices.
