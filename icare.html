Frosfood Reserve – Payment Consent

  • Frosfood Reserve is a structured food allocation system and not a savings, thrift, or investment platform.
  • Your monthly payment is a contribution toward your December Food Reserve allocation.
  • Contributions made before the Allocation Lock Date (End of Q2) may be refunded minus a 10% Early Exit Administrative and Allocation Reversal Fee.
  • After the Allocation Lock Date (End of Q2), all contributions are committed to secured inventory procurement and are not refundable.
  • Members who do not complete full contributions by the final contribution deadline will receive a proportional food allocation based strictly on total contributions made relative to the full package value.
  • Late payments may not benefit from early price protection.
  • Item substitutions may occur in cases of supplier shortages or price volatility while maintaining equivalent value.
  • Doorstep delivery applies only during the official distribution window and within designated service areas.
